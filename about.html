Bio

Career long software developer having ventured into the construction industry working for a civil engineering firm as a multi-role professional performing the tasks of a project coordinator, civil designer, and IT administrator. In my spare time, I seek freelance software development opportunities where I can discover how others are looking to add value to their product or business using software and apply (and sharpen) my broad technology skills to help them translate those requirements into a solution that exceeds expectations.

Here is some of my professional IT experience by role:

  • As a business intelligence developer I worked with databases and transactional information systems while combining and developing a hosts of skills and knowledge to optimize the flow of data within organizations. My role was to bridge the gap between business users and (the critical company data housed in their technical infrastructure)the technical infrastructure containing critical data about the company. In addition to the exposure of working in various business and technical environments, I enjoyed designing datamarts and creating ETL processes.
  • As a mobile application developer I have developed and published Android and iPhone mobile applications. My professional experience comes in the way of providing mobile access to existing business web applications using native mobile platform SDKs and integrating REST APIs services in to the server side architecture.
  • As a software engineer I have worked on all phases of the software development lifecycle for business applications primarily developing specialized web applications for small companies. I have enjoyed working on all aspects of the development stack but found that my strengths lie in working on the server side portion of web applications.
  • As a web developer, particularly web application development, I have become proficient with tools, languages, scripts, server-side programming languages and frameworks to create complex web applications. My professional experience involves designing the layout and navigation as well as developing front-end and back-end aspects of websites and applications.
